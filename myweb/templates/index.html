The name ‘LEGO’ is an abbreviation of the two Danish words “leg godt”, meaning “play well”.


On March 5th 2022 LEGO has officially opened its corporate headquarters in Billunds, Denmark. This marked end of the first phase of construction with new phase planned to start in 2023.
